Small rounded pill for counts, categories, plan tiers, and inline tags.

```jsx
<Badge tone="primary">12 voices</Badge>
<Badge tone="signal" icon="badge-check">Consented</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" size="sm">Compromised</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent">AI voice</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral · primary · accent · signal · success · warning · danger · solid`. For live/status dots use **StatusPill** instead.
